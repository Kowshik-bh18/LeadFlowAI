LEADFLOW AI – INCIDENT RESPONSE POLICY
Version: 2.0 | Effective: January 2024 | Owner: CISO

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines how LeadFlow AI detects, responds to, and recovers from security incidents and data breaches affecting customer and company data.

2. INCIDENT CLASSIFICATION
P0 (Critical): Active data breach, ransomware, or system-wide outage. SLA: Response within 15 minutes, customer notification within 1 hour.
P1 (High): Confirmed unauthorized access attempt with potential data exposure. SLA: Response within 1 hour, customer notification within 4 hours.
P2 (Medium): Failed intrusion attempt, suspicious activity detected. SLA: Response within 4 hours.
P3 (Low): Policy violation, minor anomaly. SLA: Response within 24 hours.

3. RESPONSE TEAM
The Incident Response Team (IRT) is led by the CISO and includes: VP Engineering, Legal Counsel, Head of Customer Success, and PR Lead. The IRT is on-call 24/7 via PagerDuty. Escalation path: On-call engineer → Engineering Manager → VP Engineering → CISO.

4. DETECTION AND ALERTING
Security events are detected via AWS GuardDuty, CloudTrail anomaly detection, Datadog SIEM, and WAF rule triggers. PagerDuty alerts are sent to on-call staff within 5 minutes of alert trigger.

5. CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES
Upon P0/P1 detection: Affected systems are isolated immediately via security group rule changes. API access is revoked for suspected compromised accounts. Customer data access is suspended for the affected tenant pending investigation.

6. CUSTOMER NOTIFICATION
For confirmed data breaches affecting customer data, LeadFlow AI will notify affected customers within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33. Notifications include: nature of breach, data categories affected, likely consequences, and mitigation measures. A dedicated incident status page is maintained at status.leadflow.ai.

7. POST-INCIDENT REVIEW
All P0 and P1 incidents require a blameless post-mortem within 5 business days. Post-mortems are shared with affected enterprise customers upon request. Root cause analysis (RCA) documents are retained for 3 years.

8. BUSINESS CONTINUITY
LeadFlow AI maintains a Business Continuity Plan (BCP) reviewed annually. Failover to DR region (eu-west-1) can be initiated within 30 minutes for P0 outages.
